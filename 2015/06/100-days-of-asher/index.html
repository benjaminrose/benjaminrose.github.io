100 Days of Asher

By Benjamin Rose 2015-06-16

My son turned 100 (days) yesterday. As a retrospective my wife and I took a photo (or something else) from each day and made a video. Viewing the completed project I was shocked to see how much he has grown, developed and come into his own personality1.
