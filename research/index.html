Research

I am interested in observational cosmology and Type Ia supernovae.

Currently I am looking for correlations between the properties of Type Ia supernovae and their host galaxies with Hubble Space Telescope (HST) data.

Previously I worked on a search for a cosmic bulk flow. This paper has been submitted and a preprint is available on arXiv:1412.1529. We look for directional dependence to the Hubble residuals. If this cosmic bulk flow exists then it might be a remnant from before or during inflation.
