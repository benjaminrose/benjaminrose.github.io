About Me

I am a graduate student at the University of Notre Dame perusing my Ph.D. in Physics. Other then my Ph.D. work, I enjoy working with technology and expressing my creativity.

Science

I do research in the area of Type Ia supernovae and their use in observational cosmology with my advisor Peter Garnavich. We just finishing a paper on searching for a cosmic bulk flow and are starting work with HST images looking at the effects of host galaxies properties on Type Ia’s. If you want to know more you can look at my research.

Technology

I mostly code in python,1 but I know a bit of Mathematica, Matlab, and other languages. I code to get my work done and have had very little formal training. I love learning more and if you work with a Mac (or any Unix system) I would highly recommend leancrew, bartb.ie, and ttscoff.2 For a more complete list please visit my cv.

Creativity

When I am not in front of a computer, I will often be behind a camera. I love taking photos. If you would like to see them then go to flickr. I am also a musician, married to a Theatre major, and dance.

Miscellaneous

I am a first time expectant father, waiting for my son to be born in March 2015! Also my adorable dog does amazing work volunteering as a Therapy dog.
