About Me

I am a graduate student at the University of Notre Dame perusing my Ph.D. in physics. Other then my Ph.D. work I enjoy working with technology and expressing my creativity.

Science

I work in Type Ia supernovae with my advisor Peter Garnavich. We are finishing a paper on searching for a cosmic bulk flow with Type Ia Supernovae and will soon start working with HST images looking at the effects of host galaxies on Ia properties. If you want to know more you can read my cv (that will posted soon).

Technology

I code in python. I know a bit of Mathematica, Matlab, and other languages. I code to get my work done and have had very little formal training.
